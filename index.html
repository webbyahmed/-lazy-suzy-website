Our Story

A little yellow house where time slows down

Tucked behind a tree that grows right through the roof, Lazy Suzy is Indiranagar's most beloved all-day brunch spot. Our ex-Oberoi chefs bring classic European techniques to a cozy, come-as-you-are setting.

Order a cappuccino. Open a book. You won't be rushed — and that's the whole point.
